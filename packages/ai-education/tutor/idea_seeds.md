# Idea Seeds — Reverse Traceability Log

Records which critiques and open questions from paper reading sessions were promoted to the JMP Idea vault via `/paper-done`.

## Format

```
[YYYY-MM-DD] paper: <paper title> | source: <slug>.md
  → appended to: <existing idea slugs, or "none">
  → created: <new idea slugs, or "none">
  → skipped: <N> items
```
