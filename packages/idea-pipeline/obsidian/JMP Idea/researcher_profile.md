## Methodology Requirements (Hard Filters)

**Must have at least one of:**
- Randomized controlled trial (RCT) with clean randomization
- Difference-in-differences with credible parallel trends
- Instrumental variable with explicit defense of exclusion restriction
- Regression discontinuity
- Structural model with calibrated parameters and counterfactual simulations
- Well-identified event study

**Hard reject if:**
- No identification strategy at all (pure descriptive without causal claim)
- IV where the instrument is itself an equilibrium outcome
- Topic is outside your core research area (customize this list for your field)

## Research Quality Bar for New Ideas

An idea is worth pursuing if it has **all three**:
1. **Specific causal mechanism**: not "X affects Y" but "X affects Y *through* Z channel, because W"
2. **Defensible identification strategy articulable at proposal stage**: can say *today* what the exogenous variation is
3. **Accessible data source**: can name a specific dataset that exists and can be obtained
